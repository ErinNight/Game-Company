About Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game (RPG) developed and published by Hoyoverse. Launched in September 2020, in which it quickly became a well known game thanks to its amazing open-world exploration capabilites with its captivating storylines and engaging gameplay mechanics. Set in the world of Teyvat, Genshin Impact allows players to explore seven regions, each with their own cultures, environments, and elements.
